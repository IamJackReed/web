Introducing the MGA-1 Multi-gas Sorbent Analyzer

Test methods include isotherms, adsorption time constants, and thermal cycling for aging and stability. The system leverages compact sensors and flow cells to accelerate test times up to 20x faster than other techniques

The MGA-1 measures CO and HO adsorption using two sensors: a gravimetric sensor for total uptake and a capacitance sensor for polar molecule (e.g. HO) uptake
